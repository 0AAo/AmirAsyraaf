Street Fighter 6

Platforms: PS5, PS4, Xbox Series X/S, PC
Due: June 2th, 2023

Fighting games feel like that specialist dying breed, but if anything’s going to reinvigorate the genre then Street Fighter 6 is going to be the one. With a bold hyper-real style and a more accessible control option, early hands-on already reveal a supremely polished fighting game with exciting new systems.
